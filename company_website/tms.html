Tournament Management System


Manage tournaments with ease

With our powerful core management software it makes it easy for you to arrange tournaments, and for the participants to register. There's no for hassling with entering participants manually and the system can automatically set registration time limits, and limits on how many people can register for a event.

Scheduling

Staying overtime for scheduling a tournament? No more! With our scheduling package the tournament automatically gets scheduled at the end of the registration period without any need for your staff to interfere.

Built to scale and extend

Our software is built to be able to handle high load, and to scale as your company and tournaments do. There's no tournament too big. We always design with extendability in mind and thus we do all we can to make sure it's as fast and easy as possible to extend our core software so you always can get a module you specifically need.

Sell tickets

As a company we appreciate the need to have a solid source of income. With our system you can choose to sell tickets to spectators of your event with no hassle, and with your payment gateway of choice.
